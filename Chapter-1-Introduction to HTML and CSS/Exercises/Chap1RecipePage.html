Cinnamon Roll

These are classic, home-made cinnamon rolls, simple and easy for beginners! Cinnamon rolls are also great to make a head of time when inviting guests over!

Ingredients:

  • 240ml Warm Milk
  • 63g Granualted Sugar
  • 7g yeast
  • 580g Bread/AP Flour
  • 183g Brown Sugar
  • 9g Salt
  • 24g cinnamon
  • 115g unsalted melted butter
  • 14ml vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 85g of Room Temp. Butter
  • 80g Cocoa Powder

Method:

  1. In a bowl, add 240ml of warm milk, 5 tbsp of granulated sugar and 2 and 1 quarter grams of active dry yeast and mix before letting it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, add 580g of bread flour, 80g of brown sugar, 1 and 1/5 tsp of salt and a 1/2 tsp of cinnamon before mixing and adding it to the first bowl.
  3. Back in the first bowl, add 115g of salted melted butter, 1 large egg and 1 tbsp of vanilla extract before using a mixer on low speed until it becomes a shaggy mixture. Then, increase the speed and mix until it becomes smooth, silky, and doesn't stick to your fingers.
  4. Add the dough to a well oiled bowl and let it rest in a warm place until doubled in size.
  5. Put the risen dough on a lightly flour'd counter and roll it out into a rectangle by patting and shaping it.
  6. Create a dry mixture of cinnamon, brown sugar, and cocoa powder. The ratio of the ingredients can be adjusted to your preference.
  7. Spread out some warm, but not melting, butter over it evenly before covering it with the mixture.
  8. Roll the dough as evenly as you can and cut the roll into smaller pieces.
  9. Add the pieces to a tray with plenty of space in between as they will grow in size in the oven.
  10. Take it out when golden and colour and enjoy while its warm!
